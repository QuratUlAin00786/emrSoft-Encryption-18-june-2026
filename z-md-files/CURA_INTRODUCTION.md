# Cura EMR System
## Modern Healthcare Management Platform

---

### Overview

**Cura** is a comprehensive, cloud-based Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system designed to modernize healthcare operations. Built by Cura Software Limited, Cura combines traditional medical record management with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver an intelligent, efficient, and compliant healthcare solution.

---

### What is Cura?

Cura is a multi-tenant healthcare platform that enables medical organizations to:

- Manage complete patient records digitally
- Schedule and track appointments efficiently
- Process prescriptions with safety checks
- Conduct telemedicine consultations
- Generate lab results and medical reports
- Communicate securely with patients and staff
- Maintain full regulatory compliance (UK GDPR)

---

### Key Features

#### **Patient Care Management**
- Complete electronic health records (EHR)
- Medical history, allergies, and medication tracking
- NHS integration with patient identification
- Health risk scoring and monitoring
- Insurance and billing management

#### **Clinical Operations**
- Multi-provider appointment scheduling
- Digital prescription management with drug interaction alerts
- Laboratory test ordering and results tracking
- Medical imaging and radiology reports
- Customizable clinical forms and documentation

#### **AI-Powered Intelligence**
- Clinical decision support using GPT-4o
- Automated risk assessment and treatment suggestions
- Intelligent chatbot for appointment booking
- Drug interaction analysis with confidence scoring
- Facial muscle analysis for aesthetic treatments

#### **Communication & Collaboration**
- Real-time team messaging system
- SMS and WhatsApp patient notifications
- Video conferencing for telemedicine
- Email integration for automated communications
- Multi-channel notification management

#### **Security & Compliance**
- Organization-level data isolation
- Role-based access control with 16 default roles
- UK GDPR compliance framework
- Comprehensive audit trails
- Secure JWT authentication
